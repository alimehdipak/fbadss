Just love these furry friends more than I think.
\n<\/p>\n

This is the case of Buckshot the horse. The playful animal is allowed to attend the maternity shoot of his mother, Amanda. Buckshot is always a good boy but this time, he clears up his title himself. He \u201cruins\u201d the emotional photoshoot with his biggest teeth smile ever. And this is incredibly hilarious!<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

What did the horse do? The horse couldn\u2019t hide his excitement for the shoot. He stood next to his dad, Phil, spotted the camera, and put the biggest smile on it. What a hysterical moment!<\/p>\n

And, of course, the horse\u2019s smile won the spotlight of the maternity shoot. It was captured through the lens of photographer Kristen Zaffiro. It went viral in an instant when shared on the Internet. People agree that the horse was born with an excellent sense of humor.
\n<\/p>\n

Amanda was in the last week of her pregnancy. She wanted to run a photoshoot to save this amazing period. And her well-mannered horses deserved to get a spot on the scene. But the cheerful Buckshot made all people around laugh out loud.
\n<\/p>\n

\u201cThis photo session\u2026 I laughed so hard! Who knew when I told THIS horse to smile, he would! Best maternity session ever,\u201d he wrote in a now viral post!
\nHis big teeth smile really turned her maternity photoshoot into a memorable experience. He spoiled it but did it in the cutest and sweetest ways. It\u2019s always great to have friendly four-legged companions around you.
