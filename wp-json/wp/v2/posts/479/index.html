As parents, nothing is happier than seeing their baby come into this world healthily. If you have been waiting for the arrival of your baby for a long time, the happiness will double up. You burst into tears with happiness and thankfulness. That moment fills empty parts of your soul, and surely, you will never forget it.
\n<\/p>\n

This can also be seen in animals, guys. An old pelican couple couldn\u2019t be happier to see their egg hatch. Their precious chick finally comes after 6 years of waiting.<\/p>\n

Mr. Percival (name of the male pelican) was rescued by the Twinnies Pelican and Seabird Rescue in Australia. They spotted him on Chambers Island in Maroochydore, Queensland 18 years ago.
\n<\/p>\n

The poor little pelican got trapped in fishing lines and hook, causing bad injuries on his wings. He ended up losing his wings but luckily, he grew up in love and care of the staff and volunteers at the sanctuary which got a permit to keep him.<\/p>\n

The gentle pelican has lived at the sanctuary since then. He met his beloved partner and they have desired to have a baby.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

For six years, the couple has spent time nesting and caring for their eggs. However, luck didn\u2019t smile upon them because none of the eggs hatched. They were upset but they didn\u2019t give up on it.<\/p>\n

The staff at the sanctuary saw this and decided to do an amazing thing for the pelicans. They placed a fellow pelican\u2019s egg in their nest. And, after years of persistent waiting, the pair was finally gifted a perfect little baby chick.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

That moment was extremely special and magical. The new dad and mom couldn\u2019t hide their excitement. They got their own baby.
\n\u201cMr. Percival has never given up trying to have a baby pelican chick. He sits on his eggs with his partner that shares the incubation and the eggs have never hatched. He has been doing this for 6 years now and we felt so sorry for him as it\u2019s so sad when he sees the others around him seeing baby pelicans,\u201d reads the post on the Twinnies Pelican and Seabird Rescue Facebook page.<\/p>\n


\nMr. Percival proves to be a patient and responsible dad. He feeds the chick more often than his partner does. Both love spending time sitting on their baby. The family will have a lot of memorable experiences together.<\/p>\n

\u201cHe made us cry! As you can tell, he and his partner are in love with their newborn pelican chick. Glad he will feel like the others that are surrounded around him with their pelican chicks,\u201d the carers share their excitement.
\n<\/p>\n
