Giving birth is one of the most incredible moments a mom can ever experience. It\u2019s also one of the most secretive, at least for human moms \u2013 they\u2019re always squirreled away in a private hospital room, away from prying eyes.<\/p>\n

Usually it\u2019s mom, dad, and random hospital staff that first greet the baby as he or she comes bursting forth into the world. But for wild elephants, it\u2019s an entirely different story. The whole herd is there to say hello to their newest little tribe member!<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

In this video clip, a group of lucky tourists on safari actually got to witness the intimate meet-and-greet as it unfolded. People go on safari so they can get the chance to observe mother nature up close and personal, so of course there was tons of excitement in their voices when they realize they\u2019re getting exactly what they came to see!<\/p>\n

In this real-life nature documentary, a mama elephant is standing out in the middle of the grasslands, and she\u2019s right in the middle of giving birth in the drizzling rain. The rest of her herd is keeping a safe distance, because even in the great outdoors mom still needs some personal space and room to breathe! Then, before you know it, her adorable pipsqueak pops out into the world.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

There always comes a moment in a brand new mom\u2019s life where she wants to proudly show off her offspring. In this case, the happy mama didn\u2019t have to wait for very long.<\/p>\n

It seemed as if everyone from the baby\u2019s great grandma to his second cousins were there to celebrate! The elephant herd quickly surrounded both mom and the baby calf to ooh and aah over the cutie pie, and congratulate mom on a job well done.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

But, sensing that tourists were nearby, the herd also formed a protective barrier around the vulnerable little guy. All the adult elephants were very vocal in letting the humans know that they should stay back \u2013 way back!<\/p>\n

Elephants are very sensitive and emotional creatures, and it\u2019s very obvious from this footage that they\u2019re just like humans when it comes to welcoming their babies into the world. There\u2019s lots of love and elephant trunk high-fives to go around, but they\u2019re also very protective of their little ones, too.<\/p>\n
