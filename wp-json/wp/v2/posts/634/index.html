Anyone who has ever owned a Cogi knows that they are funny, cuddly, and charming creatures. The name corgi literally means \u201cdwarf dog\u201d in Welsh. They have that name because they are famous for their miniature legs and tiny tails. Corgis are great with kids, are extremely loyal, and are known to be eager to please. Most of us like them because they seem like such goofy little sweet things.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Meet Baby, a 5-month-old Corgi who never fails to make everyone around her smile. May Theint Nwe, Baby\u2019s owner, got her after falling in love with their first corgi, Cooper.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The two corgis become best friends and live happily with their owners. Their humans even created an Instagram account for them to share their cute photos and pictures with the world. They want to show everyone just how much fun and lovely these goofballs are.
\n<\/p>\n

\u201cCooper is the first dog that we got and he is 2 years old now. He\u2019s a Pembroke Welsh Corgi,\u201d May Theint Nwe introduced her pet to Bored Panda. He is a kind and sweet boy to everyone and a good big brother to Baby.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

\u201cBaby is our second puppy and she\u2019s also a Pembroke Welsh Corgi. She does, however, have a slightly fluffier coat than her big brother. She is very playful and feisty. She sometimes gets jealous [of Cooper] if she doesn\u2019t get enough attention herself.\u201d<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

But no matter how playful and feisty she is, she still loves everyone, including her big brother!<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

According to their owner, Cooper doesn\u2019t like posing for photos as much as his sister does, so their Instagram account is full of Baby\u2019s photos.<\/p>\n

Cooper definitely is an important member of the family, but this time we would like to spend some time with his baby sister, Baby! Scroll down to see her photos!
\nIf you love the two corgis, you can follow them on Facebook or Instagram to see more their cute pics and videos!<\/p>\n
