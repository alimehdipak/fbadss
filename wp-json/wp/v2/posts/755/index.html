There are only a few animals on the planet more adorable than baby goats. And, you can\u2019t find many dogs that are more affectionate than golden retrievers. What happens if these two species get on and form a family? They are the cutest gang that you can raise. Your home will never lack smiles and fun when having these joyful furry friends.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Andrea Holley is the owner of this odd, cute gang. She has an intelligent and patient golden retriever named Loryn. When the woman adopted a few rescued baby goats, Loryn fell in love with the kids and took care of them as her own.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Loryn has grown up on her family\u2019s farm. She deserves to be called a \u201cgood girl\u201d as she always helps her mom watch on and care for all of the animals. The dog shows her mother\u2019s instinct to all baby animals she meets on the farm. She just wants to spread love and comfort to them.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

\u201cLoryn is my right-hand man and always helps corral the babies when we\u2019re outside or round them up while playing inside,\u201d Holley said. \u201cShe most definitely thinks they are her babies,\u201d Andrea Holley said.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

When Holley brought a few rescued baby goats to their farm, Loryn volunteered to babysit the kids. She was so happy with the arrival of the goats and couldn\u2019t stop cuddling them.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

As the goats were still very young, Holley placed them in a playpen inside her house to bottle-feed them. And this time, Loryn patiently waited outside the enclosure until the goats finished their meals. She never let them out of her sight.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n
